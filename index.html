About Me

I'm a Senior UX Designer (4+ years), based in Bangalore, currently at Tata Elxsi. I design complex digital systems where clarity, trust, and decision-making matter, turning messy workflows into experiences that feel intuitive and purposeful.

I've worked across large-scale airport experiences and enterprise, data-heavy platforms bringing structure, clarity, and strong visual craft to high-impact work. From wayfinding and media systems to SaaS dashboards and internal tools, I focus on making information easier to scan, compare, and act on.

I’m especially drawn to dashboards and information design, with a focus on accessibility, ownership, and outcomes. I enjoy defining reusable patterns, building design-system thinking into products, and collaborating closely with engineering to ship polished details. Lately, I’m exploring how AI can enhance research and design workflows without losing the human intent or usability.
