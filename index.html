About Me

Designing with empathy,
shipping with precision.

I'm a Senior UX Designer (4+ years), based in Bangalore, currently at Tata Elxsi. I design complex digital systems where clarity, trust, and decision-making matter — turning messy workflows into experiences that feel intuitive and purposeful.

I've worked across large-scale airport experiences and enterprise, data-heavy platforms bringing structure, clarity, and strong visual craft to high-impact work.

I'm especially drawn to dashboards and information design, with a focus on accessibility, ownership, and outcomes. Lately, I'm exploring how AI can enhance research and design workflows without losing human intent.

  • Creating accessible experiences
