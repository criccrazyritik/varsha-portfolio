About Me

I'm a Senior UX Designer with 4+ years of experience designing complex, large-scale digital systems where clarity and decision-making matter. Based in Bangalore, I work at Tata Elxsi, solving high-impact problems by turning complexity into intuitive, visually strong, and purposeful experiences.

My journey is defined by the kind of responsibility I take on. I've contributed to award-winning projects like Guwahati Airport, led the Lucknow Airport Media and Digital Experience initiative, and played a critical role in Navi Mumbai International Airport— projects that operate at national scale and serve millions of users. Alongside this, I've worked on enterprise and data-intensive platforms, including my journey with Comcast, where precision and structure were non-negotiable.

What drives my work is the power of visual thinking. I have a strong affinity for data presentation — dashboards, graphs, and systems that help people understand, decide, and act. I bring a balance of curiosity and seriousness to my practice, with a deep commitment to accessibility, ownership, and outcomes. As an AI SPOC for UX, I actively explore how emerging technologies can elevate design workflows while keeping human intent at the center.

What drives me

  • Exploring AI in design
